Profile

Will Power — Construction Landscaper

I am a dedicated civil trades professional with over a decade of experience across landscape construction, earthworks, and infrastructure. My career began in landscaping, building a foundation in site preparation, drainage, irrigation, plumbing, paving, and stonemasonry — skills I still bring to every outdoor project.

At Garden Partner, an award-winning landscaping company, I specialised in high-end residential and commercial landscapes where quality, precision, and craftsmanship were paramount. Over nearly a decade I became a versatile tradesman and supervisor, leading crews to deliver projects that set industry benchmarks.

Today I combine that landscaping craft with leadership and heavy plant expertise — ready to deliver premium outdoor environments from concept through construction.
